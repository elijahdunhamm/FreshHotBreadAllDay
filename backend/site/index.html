    Our Story

    A Tradition of Excellence

    Señorita Bread is a Filipino bread, and my wife Liza is from the Philippines. She has been making it for over twenty years in California. She has made the recipe her own by tweaking it to what we serve now. A lot of love goes into our bread everyday.

    Every loaf is a testament to Liza's commitment to excellence. We use only the finest ingredients, carefully selected for their quality and taste. Our bakers wake up early every morning to ensure that each piece of Señorita Bread is perfect.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do you take large orders for events?

    Yes — we accommodate large orders. Contact us in advance for custom quantities and pickup arrangements.

    Do you offer delivery?

    Yes we offer both pickup and delivery options. Contact us for more details.

    Can I request custom packaging for gifts?

    Absolutely — let us know at the time of order and we will prepare a nice gift-ready package.

    How far in advance should I order?

    For regular orders, same-day pickup is usually available. For large orders (50+ pieces), we recommend ordering at least 24 hours in advance.
